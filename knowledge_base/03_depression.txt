Feeling Low or Depressed
If you feel hopeless, empty, or everything feels pointless for more than 2 weeks:
 This is NOT weakness. Depression is a medical condition.
 Reach out immediately — talking helps 100%
Indian helplines (247, free, confidential):
 iCall (TISS): 9152987821
 Vandrevala Foundation: 9999666555
 Jeevan Aastha (Gujarat): 18002333330
 Emergency: 112 or 100
You deserve help. Taking the first step is bravery.
